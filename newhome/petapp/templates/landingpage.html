Take care

We care for your pet


     Welcome to our pet shop, where we offer a unique service tailored to meet the needs of both pets and their owners. Are you planning a vacation or a trip? Leave your worries behind and entrust the care of your beloved furry friends to us. At our pet shop, we provide comprehensive care for pets while their owners are away.

Our dedicated team ensures that each pet receives personalized attention and care throughout their stay. For just 500 per day, your pet will enjoy a comfortable and safe environment, complete with nutritious meals, regular health check-ups, and access to veterinary services if needed.

But that's not all – we go the extra mile to keep your pets happy and healthy. Our experienced staff takes dogs for walks, engages them in fun activities, and even provides training sessions to keep them mentally and physically stimulated.

We understand that every pet is unique, which is why we prioritize individualized care for each furry guest. Whether your pet is a social butterfly or a shy introvert, we tailor our services to meet their specific needs and preferences.

So, why worry about your pet's well-being while you're away? Contact us today to learn more about our services and give your pet the vacation they deserve. At our pet shop, we're committed to providing top-notch care and ensuring that both you and your pet have peace of mind.
